3-1Leads.com

In hockey, a 3-1 lead is commonly regarded as the worst lead to have in a game or series. This extends to other sports like basketball or baseball, and some even reference the bible. I was curious what the actual statistics were like for hockey, so I put this together to try and aggregate that data.

I accidentally let the domain lapse, so I no longer have it. I will update this with a link to the new domain when I get one.
