About Me

If you're on my site, you probably know that I really enjoy hockey! When I graduated from MSOE, I moved to Cleveland, and at that point I started following hockey again with some of my newfound free time. Obviously I didn't expect to buy a ton of jerseys, but there were several specialty jersey nights for the Cleveland Monsters, and the replicas for sale were a lot more affordable than most jerseys tend to be, and then all of a sudden here we are.

Feel free to peruse my collection of jerseys - and let me know which one is your favorite lol
